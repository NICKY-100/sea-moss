Supports overall health and wellness

Sea moss is a remarkable whole food, naturally packed with an unparalleled mineral punch. It contains 92 out of the 102 minerals our bodies need. This exceptional superfood provides nearly every mineral essential for optimal nutritional support and improved health. Rich in iron, magnesium, potassium iodide, and calcium, this magical marine vegetable offers numerous benefits.

Promotes weight loss and improves metabolism

It contains iodine which is important for the thyroid and metabolic functions of our body. The role of sea moss in Basal metabolic rate (no. of calories required by the body at rest) benefits our body’s ability to process calories which ultimately keeps our weight in check.

Boosts your immune function

Sea moss is an effective superfood to strengthen your immune system. It is believed to possess some extraordinary properties that include it being antibacterial, antiviral and anti-inflammatory. These properties of red seaweed make it effective against infections and help quicken the body’s natural healing system.

Supports pregnancy

The nutritious moss has folate, zinc, and iodine. All these nutrients are required by the human body in their optimal amounts to help conception.

Post-workout recovery

Sea moss is made up of collagen proteins which are useful in strengthening and repairing the connective tissues. The anti-inflammatory properties make it effective in post-workout recovery. Also, the mucilaginous properties can provide relief and lubricate the joints (in case you experience muscle or joint pain during/post-workout).
