Sea Moss

Sea moss, also known as Irish moss, is a type of red algae that grows along the Atlantic coasts of North America and Europe. It is rich in vitamins and minerals, including iodine, calcium, and magnesium, making it a popular superfood. Sea moss is often consumed in gel form, added to smoothies, soups, and desserts for its thickening properties and nutritional benefits. Additionally, it is used in skincare products for its moisturizing and soothing qualities. Overall, sea moss is valued for its health benefits and versatility in both culinary and cosmetic applications.
